## Introduction
Cammy.io is a Discord bot designed to create a captivating Pokémon RPG experience right in your server. With interactive gameplay, battles, and a growing world of Pokémon, your members can explore, train, and compete in an immersive universe.

## Features
- Catch wild Pokémon and build your team.
- Engage in battles against NPCs and other trainers.
- Customize your profile and display your achievements.
- Explore different regions and discover rare Pokémon.
- Evolve your Pokémon through training and experience.
- Complete quests and challenges to earn rewards.
- and much more!

## Installation
1. Invite Cammy.io to your server by [clicking here]([#](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1141577311364337724&scope=bot&permissions=0)).
2. Grant the necessary permissions for Cammy.io to function properly.
3. Configure the bot's settings as per your server's preferences (see [Configuration](#configuration)).

## Usage
Once installed, Cammy.io offers a range of commands and interactions to navigate the Pokémon world. Use `/start` to begin your adventure and follow the prompts to create your trainer and choose a starter Pokémon.

## Commands
- `/start`: Begin your Pokémon adventure.
- `/profile`: View your trainer profile and Pokémon team.
- `/catch [pokemon]`: Attempt to catch a wild Pokémon.
- `/battle [opponent]`: Challenge an opponent to a battle.
- `/evolve [pokemon]`: Evolve your Pokémon into its next form.
- `/quests`: Check available quests and challenges.
- `/region [name]`: Explore different regions and discover Pokémon.
- **MOST OF THESE ARE NOT READY YET!**

Check in-bot help or use `/help [command]` to get detailed information about specific commands.

## Configuration
To configure Cammy.io for your server, you can modify settings such as:
- Starting regions and Pokémon.
- Battle mechanics and AI difficulty.
- Experience and leveling system.
- Available quests and rewards.
- Bot prefixes and command settings.

Refer to the bot's documentation or in-bot help for details on how to customize these settings.
